What are Cats?

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility, playfulness, and hunting skills.

Natural Habitat

Cats can live in forests, grasslands, and even cities. Domestic cats easily adapt to living with humans.

Breeds and Types

Popular breeds include Persian, Siamese, Bengal, and Maine Coon. Each breed has unique features and personality.

Love for Humans

Cats show love by purring, rubbing, and staying close to their owners. They are loyal and comforting companions.
